I am a software engineer focused on backend systems, cloud infrastructure, and applied AI. Over the years, I have built production systems across web platforms, distributed services, and data-driven applications, with a strong emphasis on reliability, clarity, and execution.

Today, my work sits at the intersection of software engineering, site reliability, and AI/ML systems. I design and build applications that turn unstructured data into structured intelligence, and I am especially interested in agentic architectures, LLM-powered workflows, and robust backend platforms.

My technical background includes Python, FastAPI, AWS, Docker, Terraform, distributed systems, and backend architecture. I care about building software that is not only functional, but maintainable, observable, and commercially useful.

I studied Philosophy, which shaped how I think: clearly, carefully, and with attention to first principles. I write to share what I learn, refine my thinking, and document ideas worth building.
